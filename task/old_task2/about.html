About Us:

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is a global IT services, consulting, and business solutions organization. Operating as the flagship company of the Tata Group, TCS helps global enterprises transform their businesses through innovative technologies, including a heavy focus on cloud modernization, artificial intelligence, and digital transformation.

TCS was founded on April 1, 1968.

  • Founder: J. R. D. Tata (Jehangir Ratanji Dadabhoy Tata) established the company under Tata Sons.
  • Father of Indian IT: F. C. Kohli (Fakir Chand Kohli) was the first general manager and the driving force behind the company's formation and initial software services.

Company Mission Vision and Values :

  1. Mission: To help customers achieve their business objectives by providing innovative, best-in-class consulting, IT solutions, and services, while making it enjoyable for all stakeholders to work with the organization.

  2. Vision: To be one of the top 10 global corporate houses, bringing technology-driven business transformations to clients worldwide.

  3. Values : The operations and conduct of TCS are guided by five fundamental values("Leading change, Integrity, Respect for the individual, Excellence, Learning and sharing.")
