Building the Perpetually Adaptive Enterprise

Building perpetually adaptive enterprises by applying technology, AI, and intelligence at scale.

Rooted in the heritage of the Tata Group, TCS is a premier global technology and digital transformation partner. Since 1968, the company has delivered innovative engineering and customer service to organizations worldwide.

TCS offers a comprehensive suite of IT and business solutions:

Custom application development, cloud infrastructure, and enterprise software implementation.

Aggregates Generative AI platforms (like TCS AI WisdomNext) and machine learning capabilities for enterprises.

End-to-end management of finance, HR, and supply chain operations.

Strategic tech consulting to drive organizational change.

Core platforms such as TCS BaNCS (for financial institutions) and TCS MasterCraft.
