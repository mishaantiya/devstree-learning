Hello, puppy!

A little puppy is a bundle of joy and energy. These sweet baby dogs are defined by their soft fur, big clumsy paws, and curious personalities. They require lots of sleep, nutritious food, and patient training to grow into happy, healthy adult dogs.



Big Expressive Eyes

Puppies are famous for their large, dark eyes. These eyes melt hearts and help them communicate their innocent curiosity.



Clumsy Paws

They are born with oversized paws that they haven't quite grown into yet. This makes their walking style adorable and slightly wobbly.



Soft Puppy Fur

Their coats are incredibly plush and fluffy. Petting a puppy's warm fur is a deeply comforting and soothing experience.



High Energy Zoomies

Despite their small size, puppies have bursts of endless energy. They will suddenly dash across the room or chase their own tail in pure excitement.



The Playful Bow

To ask for a game of chase or tug-of-war, a puppy will perform a "play bow". They drop their front chest to the ground while keeping their tiny tail wagging in the air.
